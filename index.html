About
Greetings everyone! I am KirbyCthulhu, and I've created this page as a way to share tools, tips, tricks and other resources with you amazing streamers out there. 

As I began my journey into streaming, I noticed there were some incredible tools out there that were watermarked or limited in order to encourage a monthly subscription.  After a bit of poking around, I found that a lot of these tools have no real complexity behind them, and I want to share my implementation with you!

So please, check out my free tools that focus on enhancing stream interactivity without hiding features behind paywalls or subscriptions!
